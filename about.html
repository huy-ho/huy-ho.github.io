Hello! I graduated from the University of California, Berkeley in December 2022 with an undergraduate degree in Data Science. I was born in Vietnam and grew up in beautiful San Diego in Southern California.

I’m fascinated by how systems are built, scaled, and kept secure. I’m especially drawn to infrastructure, cloud computing, security, AI, and consumer products.

Some of my aspirations include leading a major consumer product, working on counter-intelligence and national defense, creating the next Triple-A video game, and building large-scale cloud and infrastructure systems.

Outside of work, I play soccer, listen to all kinds of music, watch crime and spy dramas (like The Americans, Peaky Blinders, Breaking Bad, and Ozark), and enjoy cooking as an avid home chef.
